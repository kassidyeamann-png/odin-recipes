Tortellini Soup

Description

I'm a big fan of dinners like this tortellini soup that you can make ahead of time, or throw together in 30 minutes, right before you're ready to eat. That way I can buy what I need, and depending on how my days goes, I can start prepping it in the morning. Or if I don't get to it early, I know it will be easy to throw together at dinnertime. This recipe was inspired by my Creamy Tomato and Spinach Tortellini pasta that has been so popular.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch of red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 8 oz tomato sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian Seasoning
  • 8 oz cheese tortellini (the refridgerated kind)
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped

Steps

  1. Add oil and butter to a large saucepan over medium heat. Once melted, add the onion and cook, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic. Stir in the flour and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the vegetable broth, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, and dried basil. Season with salt and pepper. Taste broth and adjust seasonings if needed.
  4. Bring mixture to a gentle boil and then add the tortellini. Cook for a few minutes, until tortellini are tender.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in spinach, parmesan cheese, and cream.
  6. Add chopped basil. Serve warm.
